PhysicsLab | Magnetic Flux $\Phi$

By Rabih A.G.

System Parameters

Magnetic Field ($B$) 1.0 T
Loop Area ($S$) 1.0 m²
Angle ($\theta$) 0°
Parallel ($0^\circ$) Perpendicular ($90^\circ$)
$$ \Phi = B \cdot S \cdot \cos(\theta) $$
Instantaneous Flux: 1.00 Wb

Lab Guide

  • Blue Arrows: Magnetic Field ($\vec{B}$).
  • Red Arrow: Normal Vector ($\vec{n}$), $\perp$ to loop.
  • Green Loop: Conductive Area ($S$).
  • Flux vanishes when $\vec{n} \perp \vec{B}$ ($90^\circ$).
